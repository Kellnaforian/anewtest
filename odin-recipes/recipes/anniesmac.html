Annie's Box Mac With a Twist

There are several small ways that you can spice up your out of the box mac and cheese, this specific recipes uses Annie's Boxed Mac and Cheese

  1. The first step to an even more delicious experience is to salt the water before you cook the noodles
  2. The next step is to cook the boxed mac and cheese like normal
  3. The final step is to put an extra slice of sharp cheddar in the final product, and melt it on the stove!
